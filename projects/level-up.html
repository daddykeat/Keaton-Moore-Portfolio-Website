Level Up

Motion Graphic • After Effects • Gaming UI Animation

“Level Up” is a 10-second motion graphic inspired by modern video game user interfaces. The animation simulates progression through an experience system and resolves with a high-impact level-up reveal.

Creative Brief

Overview: A futuristic interface appears, an XP bar fills progressively, anticipation builds, and the sequence ends with a bold “LEVEL 02” reveal.

Objective: Demonstrate strong animation principles, effective effects usage, and a cohesive gaming-inspired theme.

Target Audience: Gamers, teens and young adults, digital media viewers, and audiences familiar with modern UI/UX visuals.

Visual Style: Dark minimal background, neon purple/magenta/blue accents, glowing UI elements, and cinematic polish.

Applications

  • Video game UI animation
  • Streaming overlays
  • Social media promotional content
  • Digital ads for gaming brands

Storyboard Beats

  • Intro: Futuristic interface appears on screen.
  • Beat 1: XP bar begins filling.
  • Beat 2: Glow and UI motion build anticipation.
  • Beat 3: Energy and glitch effects trigger the reveal.
  • Beat 4: “LEVEL 02” badge pops into view.
  • Outro: Particles and glow settle for the final hold.

Assets Used

  • XP bar and UI frame
  • Level 02 badge
  • Energy burst and ring
  • Particle spray
  • Lens flare
  • Glitch overlay
  • Arrow chevrons

Techniques & Effects

  • Position, scale, rotation, and opacity transforms
  • Motion blur
  • Graph editor easing
  • Glow and Fast Box Blur
  • Turbulent Displace
  • Screen/Add blending modes
  • Glitch and flash transitions

Principles of Animation

  • Timing and spacing
  • Ease in / ease out
  • Anticipation
  • Exaggeration
  • Follow-through

Process

I built this piece around the feeling of progression found in modern games. I focused on controlled pacing during the XP bar fill, then shifted into a more explosive reveal using glitch, glow, and particle effects. The final badge pop was designed to feel rewarding and polished while still keeping the interface clean and readable.

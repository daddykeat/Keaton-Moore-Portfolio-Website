Bourbon Street Awakening

Motion Graphic • After Effects • New Orleans / Mardi Gras Theme

“Bourbon Street Awakening” is a motion graphic set on Bourbon Street in New Orleans, where a glowing orb activates Mardi Gras color and light in a monochrome lamp scene.

Creative Brief

Overview: The scene begins in black and white with a quiet nighttime atmosphere. A glowing orb appears, moves through Mardi Gras beads, activates color, and then lights the lamp.

Concept & Theme: The animation represents the energy and celebration of Mardi Gras bringing life to Bourbon Street.

Visual Style: Monochrome environment with selective purple, green, and gold color activation, plus a warm glowing lamp.

Possible Applications

  • New Orleans themed video intro
  • Mardi Gras social media content
  • Motion portfolio piece
  • Event promotion visual

Storyboard Beats

  • Intro: Monochrome Bourbon Street lamp scene appears.
  • Beat 1: A glowing orb enters the frame.
  • Beat 2: The orb travels through the bead strands.
  • Beat 3: Beads activate into Mardi Gras colors.
  • Beat 4: Energy moves into the lamp and it flickers on.
  • Outro: Lamp glows fully while colorful beads stand out.

Animation Elements

  • Glowing orb
  • Bead strands
  • Color activation
  • Lamp glow
  • Light rays
  • Subtle camera zoom

Color Palette

  • Black and white environment
  • Purple, green, and gold beads
  • Warm yellow-orange lamp glow

Process

I designed this piece around transformation and atmosphere. Starting with a monochrome scene helped the glowing orb become the focal point immediately. As the orb moved through the beads, the selective color activation made the Mardi Gras theme feel symbolic and energetic. The final lamp illumination and subtle camera zoom completed the reveal.
